How we teach

The method behind every lesson.

No boring lectures. No memorising openings. Just thinking, playing, and improving.

♕

Structured, not scripted

A 5-level curriculum with 40 sessions each — but the pace and examples adapt to every student.

🔍

Puzzles before theory

Kids discover ideas through positions and puzzles first. The explanation clicks because they already found it.

🎯

Play with purpose

Every practice game has a goal — "castle before move 10", "only capture with pawns". Games become focused training.

📋

Review real games

We go through their actual games together. Finding their own mistakes teaches more than any textbook.

🙋

Patient with every kid

Shy, hyperactive, quiet, chatty — every child is different. Lessons meet them where they are.

📈

Parents stay in the loop

Summary after every class. Detailed progress report every two weeks. No guessing how they're doing.

Inside a lesson

What 45 minutes actually looks like.

0–5 min

Warm-up puzzle

A quick tactical puzzle to get the brain switched on. Matched to their current level.

5–20 min

New concept

The main lesson topic — taught through interactive positions, not slides. The student is always clicking, thinking, answering.

20–35 min

Guided play

A practice game with a specific goal related to today's topic. Coach watches and gives live nudges.

35–45 min

Review & homework

Quick review of the game they just played. Homework assigned — puzzles, a position to study, or a game to analyse.
