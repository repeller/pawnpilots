Common questions

Everything parents ask before signing up.

My child has never played chess. Is that okay?

Absolutely. The First Moves level starts from zero — how pieces move, what the board looks like, the goal of the game. No prior knowledge needed.

How long is each lesson?

40 minutes for Beginner levels (First Moves & Pattern Builder) and 45 minutes for Intermediate and Advanced levels. Short enough to stay focused, long enough to learn something real.

What do they need to join?

A laptop or tablet with a camera, a stable internet connection, and Zoom installed. That's it. We handle everything else.

How are groups organised?

Every lesson is private 1-on-1. Your child gets the coach's full attention for the entire session.

How often are the classes?

Most students attend twice a week. We'll work out a schedule that fits your family's routine when you book the trial.

Is the first class really free?

Yes, completely free with no obligation. It's a real lesson, not a sales pitch. Your child will learn something and you'll see exactly how it works.

What happens after the trial?

If your child enjoyed it, we'll recommend the right level and schedule. Monthly plans with flexible cancellation. No long-term contracts.
